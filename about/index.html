The Problem We Solve

Flight prices change constantly. Promo codes expire without warning. Hotel deals are scattered across dozens of platforms. For travelers in Singapore, Malaysia, Hong Kong, and the Philippines, finding a genuinely good deal means checking multiple sites, comparing prices, and hoping you didn't miss a flash sale.

Catavel does this work automatically. Our pipeline scans major OTAs - including Klook, Trip.com, Agoda, and Aviasales - and surfaces only the deals worth your attention. No noise, no clickbait, no fake discounts.
