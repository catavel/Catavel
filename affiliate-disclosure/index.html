Affiliate Disclosure

Catavel believes in full transparency. This page explains how we earn revenue, which companies we partner with, and how affiliate links work on our site.

In short: When you book through a link on Catavel, we may earn a small commission from the travel company - at absolutely no extra cost to you. The price you pay is the same whether you use our link or go directly to the booking site.

How Catavel Earns Revenue

Catavel is a free travel deal discovery platform. We do not charge users for access, subscriptions, or memberships. Instead, we earn revenue through affiliate partnerships with travel companies (Online Travel Agencies, airlines, eSIM providers, and other travel service providers).

When you click a link on Catavel and complete a booking or purchase on the partner's website, we receive a small commission. This commission is paid by the partner company - not by you. Your booking price remains identical to what you would pay by visiting the partner site directly.

How Affiliate Links Work

Affiliate links on Catavel look and function like regular links. When you click one, you are redirected to the partner's website with a small tracking parameter in the URL. This parameter tells the partner that the referral came from Catavel. If you complete a booking, the partner records the referral and pays us a commission - typically between 1% and 20% depending on the product category.

These tracking parameters do not collect your personal information, do not affect the price you pay, and do not install software on your device.

Our Editorial Independence

Affiliate partnerships do not influence which deals we feature, how we rank them, or what we recommend. Our deal pipeline uses an automated algorithm that selects deals based on discount percentage, route popularity, and price benchmarks - not on commission rates.

When we recommend a specific product or service (such as an eSIM provider), our recommendation is based on value, coverage, and user experience - not on which partner pays the highest commission.

What This Means for You

  • No extra cost: You never pay more by booking through Catavel. Prices are sourced directly from partners in real time.
  • Free to use: Catavel is completely free. No sign-ups, no subscriptions, no paywalls.
  • You support us: Every booking through our links helps keep Catavel running, updated, and ad-free.
  • You can opt out: If you prefer not to use affiliate links, you can visit any partner's website directly. We won't earn a commission, but you'll still find the same deals.

FTC Compliance

This disclosure is provided in accordance with the U.S. Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on affiliate marketing (16 CFR Part 255). While Catavel is based in Southeast Asia, we adhere to international best practices for transparency in affiliate marketing.
