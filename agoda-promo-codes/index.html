Frequently Asked Questions

Click “Reveal” on any code — it’s automatically copied to your clipboard and you’re redirected to Agoda. Search for your hotel, then paste the code in the “Have a coupon?” field at checkout.
Some Agoda deals don’t require a promo code — for example, app-only discounts, VIP member pricing, or “Welcome Back” deals. The discount is applied automatically when you meet the criteria. Click “Use Promo” to go directly to Agoda.
AGODATPT and similar codes need to be redeemed first on Agoda’s Deals page. Once redeemed, the coupon sits in your account and is automatically applied on your next booking within 30 days. You can use it up to 5 times.
