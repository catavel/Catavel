About Agoda

Agoda is one of Asia's leading online accommodation platforms, offering over 2 million properties including hotels, serviced apartments, villas, and resorts in 200+ countries. Founded in 2005 and headquartered in Singapore, Agoda is particularly popular among Southeast Asian travelers for its deep inventory of regional properties and competitive prices.

Agoda is the go-to platform for SG and MY travelers booking Bali villas, Bangkok hotels, and Tokyo guesthouses — often with rates not found elsewhere. Their loyalty program (Agoda Rewards) provides cashback in the form of AgodaCash redeemable on future bookings.
