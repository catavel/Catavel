Is Agoda Cheaper Than Booking.com?

It depends on the destination. Agoda generally has stronger pricing in Southeast Asia - particularly Thailand, Indonesia, Malaysia, and Vietnam - where it has deeper relationships with local properties. Booking.com tends to be stronger for Europe and North America.

For SEA travelers, checking Agoda first is usually the right move. The Secret Deals feature in particular can surface prices significantly below the standard rate - but you need to be logged in to see them.

How to Stack Agoda Discounts

Getting the lowest possible Agoda price is about layering multiple savings on top of each other. Here is how to do it step by step.

Start by creating a free Agoda account - this alone unlocks Secret Deals that are hidden from logged-out visitors. Next, build toward Genius tier status (requires 5 completed stays) which adds an automatic 10-15% discount on eligible properties on top of whatever rate you are already seeing.

Once you have AgodaCash from previous bookings, apply it as partial payment on your next stay. Then check whether your credit card has an Agoda partnership - many APAC banks do, including DBS, UOB, OCBC, and Maybank, which offer extra cashback specifically on Agoda bookings.

Finally, before confirming, cross-check the final price against Trip.com. For a broader comparison of hotel-focused deals, see our Trip.com promo codes for Malaysia page.

Agoda Promo Codes by Region

Agoda runs different promotions depending on where you are booking from and where you are going. Knowing the pattern helps you time your bookings better.

Singapore: Secret Deals are consistently the strongest discount. Credit card partnerships with DBS, OCBC, and UOB frequently offer additional cashback on top of Agoda's listed price.

Malaysia: Agoda runs MYR-specific campaigns around Hari Raya, Merdeka Day, and school holiday periods. These can be significantly deeper than standard rates and are worth waiting for if your travel dates are flexible.

Hong Kong: Strong hotel inventory for both Kowloon and Hong Kong Island, competitive with Trip.com for most property types.

Thailand and Bali: Agoda's home turf - the deepest discounts and widest property selection of any platform, especially for villas and boutique resorts that do not list elsewhere.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often does Agoda release new promo codes?
Agoda runs promotions continuously, with major campaigns around regional holidays and travel seasons. Catavel updates this page monthly with the latest verified codes. Bookmark and check back each month.
Can I use an Agoda promo code with Secret Deals?
Generally no - Secret Deals are already discounted rates and most coupon codes cannot be stacked on top. Use whichever gives you the better final price.
Does Agoda have free cancellation options?
Yes. Many properties on Agoda offer free cancellation. Always filter by "Free Cancellation" when searching if you need flexibility. Note that Secret Deals and deeply discounted rates are often non-refundable.
Does Agoda price match?
Yes, Agoda offers a Best Price Guarantee. If you find a lower price on another platform within 24 hours of booking, you can submit a claim for a refund of the difference. Terms and conditions apply.
Can I use Agoda promo codes on the mobile app?
Yes, most Agoda promo codes work on both the website and mobile app. Some codes are app-exclusive, so it is worth checking both if a code does not apply on one platform.
