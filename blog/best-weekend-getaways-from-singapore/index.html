Best Weekend Getaway from Singapore - 12 Ideas for 2026

Short trips that actually make sense from Singapore.

Singapore's greatest geographic advantage as a travel base is also its most underused: you can reach 12 genuinely different destinations within 3 hours of departure. The best weekend getaway from Singapore does not have to mean a full vacation - it can mean a Friday evening ferry to Batam, a Saturday morning flight to Bangkok, or a bus over the Causeway for a weekend of Malaysian food and shopping. This guide covers the 12 best weekend getaways from Singapore in 2026, organized by travel time so you can quickly find the right trip for your available days and budget.

Each destination includes a realistic budget estimate and the best platform to book through - because the difference between a great weekend trip from Singapore and an expensive one often comes down to knowing which tool to use.

Under 1 Hour - Ferry Getaways (Batam and Bintan)

Batam is the easiest best weekend getaway from Singapore and consistently the cheapest. The ferry from HarbourFront or Tanah Merah takes 45 minutes and runs frequently throughout the day. Book your Batam ferry via Klook for e-tickets delivered to your phone - typically SGD 20-35 one way depending on timing and operator. Batam's specific appeal: excellent cheap seafood at Golden Prawn Restaurant (a Singaporean institution), Indonesian snacks and groceries at Nagoya Hill Mall, and affordable massage treatments at a fraction of Singapore prices. A Batam day trip costs SGD 80-120 all-in including ferry, food, and shopping. An overnight stay runs around SGD 150-200 total.

Bintan is the more upscale ferry weekend getaway from SG - 1 hour from Tanah Merah Ferry Terminal to Bintan Resort Ferry Terminal. The island's north coast is dominated by resort clusters (Bintan Lagoon Resort, Angsana Bintan, Banyan Tree) that cater specifically to Singapore weekend travelers. A Bintan weekend costs significantly more than Batam: resort accommodation SGD 150-400 per night, plus ferry (SGD 40-60 return). Book the resort package via Klook or Agoda for the best rates. Under 1 Hour - Drive or Bus (Johor Bahru)

Johor Bahru (JB) is the most convenient short weekend trip from Singapore and the only one reachable without a flight or ferry. Cross the Causeway by bus (SBS 160 or Causeway Link from Queen Street Terminal, from SGD 1.50) or taxi (SGD 25-40 depending on traffic). JB's appeal is practical and specific: prices for food, shopping, and fuel are significantly lower than Singapore. LEGOLAND Malaysia and Hello Kitty Town in Nusajaya are major draws for families. City Square Mall and KSL City are the main shopping centers. For accommodation, Agoda lists budget hotels in the city center from SGD 30-60 per night. Check our Agoda Malaysia promo codes for current deals.

1-2 Hour Flights (Kuala Lumpur, Penang, Langkawi)

Kuala Lumpur is the most versatile best weekend getaway from Singapore for first-timers. A 1-hour AirAsia or Firefly flight, and KL's food scene - Jalan Alor at night, Petaling Street Chinatown, Bangsar brunch spots - is extraordinary and affordable by Singapore standards. Hotels on Agoda in Bukit Bintang run from SGD 40-80 for a comfortable 4-star. Alternatively, the KL Sentral express bus (4 hours, around SGD 25 return) via Trip.com is a budget option that avoids airport hassle.

Penang is consistently ranked among Asia's top food destinations - a 1.5-hour flight from Singapore makes it a very realistic quick getaway. George Town's heritage shophouses, Penang Hill funicular, and the Gurney Drive hawker center are the main draws. Budget SGD 250-450 for a weekend including flights and accommodation. Flights on Malindo Air or AirAsia, hotels on Agoda Secret Deals.

Langkawi offers duty-free shopping, island-hopping tours bookable via Klook, and the Langkawi Cable Car up to Gunung Mat Cincang. Beach resorts at Cenang and Tengah beaches are well-priced on Agoda. A Langkawi weekend at a mid-range beach resort runs SGD 250-500 total including flights.

2-3 Hour Flights (Bangkok, Bali, Phuket)

Bangkok is the undisputed king of the 2.5-hour weekend getaway from Singapore. Scoot and AirAsia keep fares competitive (SGD 80-150 one way on sale), and the city rewards short visits - you can see multiple temples, eat at five street food vendors, and have a Thai massage all in a single Saturday. Our Bangkok budget guide covers the full breakdown for a 3-4 day trip, including which neighbourhoods to stay in and how to get around cheaply.

Bali in 2.5 hours from Singapore is one of the great travel bargains of the region. Scoot and Garuda fly direct, and villa accommodation in Canggu or Ubud starts from SGD 35-50 per night on Agoda Secret Deals. Book your airport transfer in advance via Klook to avoid the taxi negotiation on arrival. Our full Singapore to Bali flights guide covers everything you need to book a Bali weekend from SG efficiently.

Phuket is slightly shorter at 1.5 hours from Singapore (AirAsia, Scoot). Patong and Kata beaches dominate, but Phuket Old Town is worth a morning for its Sino-Portuguese shophouses and excellent cafes. Budget SGD 350-700 for a weekend depending on whether you are staying in a guesthouse or a beach resort.

3-4 Hour Flights (Ho Chi Minh City, Hong Kong)

Ho Chi Minh City is a genuinely underrated short weekend trip from Singapore. A 2-hour Vietnam Airlines or Scoot flight, and the city delivers street food, history, and energy unlike anywhere else in SEA. The Cu Chi Tunnels day trip and Ben Thanh Market are the two must-dos. Mid-range hotels from SGD 25-45 per night. Budget SGD 280-500 for a weekend including budget flights.

Hong Kong at 3.5 hours is the most rewarding best weekend getaway from Singapore on this list. Dim sum, Disneyland, the Peak Tram, and the Star Ferry harbor crossing - all bookable via Klook at better prices than at the gate. Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest weekend trip from Singapore?
Batam is consistently the cheapest best weekend getaway from Singapore - SGD 80-150 for a day trip including ferry, food, and shopping. Johor Bahru via bus is technically the lowest cost overall but more limited in experience. For an overnight trip, JB wins on budget at SGD 80-200 total.
Which weekend getaway from Singapore is best for families?
Batam or Bintan for very young children (easy travel, resort pools). JB for LEGOLAND Malaysia and Hello Kitty Town. Bali for older children who enjoy cultural activities and outdoor adventures. Bangkok via Klook activity pre-bookings works well for teens and older kids.
Can I do a weekend trip to Bali from Singapore?
Yes, comfortably. Fly Friday evening, return Sunday evening. You get a full Saturday and Sunday morning in Bali - enough for a beach day, an Ubud excursion, and a good villa stay. Book Scoot flights 6-8 weeks ahead for the best prices and pre-book your airport transfer via Klook.
Do I need a visa for any of these destinations?
Singapore passport holders are visa-free for all 12 destinations on this list. Malaysian and Philippines passport holders should verify current requirements for Indonesia, Vietnam, and Hong Kong before booking, as policies occasionally change.
What is the best app to find cheap flights from Singapore?
Trip.com for comparing all carriers and bundling with hotels for an extra 10-15% off. Google Flights for flexible date searching and price alerts. Skyscanner for finding the cheapest calendar dates when you have schedule flexibility. Check Trip.com promo codes on Catavel before confirming any booking.
