Frequently Asked Questions

Are Klook Malaysia prices in MYR or SGD?
Klook automatically shows prices in your local currency. Malaysian users see MYR pricing by default. If you are based in Singapore and booking Malaysia activities, you can switch the currency to MYR or SGD in the app settings - the exchange rate applied is typically close to the mid-market rate.
Is Klook cheaper than buying tickets at the venue in Malaysia?
For most major attractions - yes. Sunway Lagoon, LEGOLAND, Aquaria KLCC, and Genting SkyWorlds all have consistently lower prices on Klook versus the counter. The savings range from MYR 15 to MYR 80 depending on the attraction. Smaller local attractions may have the same price on and off Klook.
Does Klook work for LEGOLAND Malaysia bookings?
Yes. LEGOLAND Malaysia in Johor Bahru is listed on Klook with multiple ticket options - Theme Park only, Waterpark only, and combo passes. The combo pass on Klook is typically MYR 30-60 cheaper than buying directly at the gate. E-tickets are delivered instantly to your Klook app.
Can I cancel a Klook Malaysia booking if my plans change?
Most Klook Malaysia activities offer free cancellation up to 24 hours before the activity date. Some peak-period bookings (like Genting on public holidays) may be non-refundable. Always check the cancellation policy on the individual listing before confirming your purchase.
Does Klook offer transfers between Singapore and Malaysia?
Yes. Klook lists private transfers and shared shuttles between Singapore (Woodlands/Jurong) and Johor Bahru, as well as coach services to KL, Penang, and Malacca. This is a convenient option for travelers who want to combine transport and attraction bookings in one platform.
