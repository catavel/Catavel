2Gardens by the Bay (Flower Dome + Cloud Forest)
⭐ 4.7 (25,000+ reviews) ⏱ 2-3 hours 🌿 Iconic attraction

Singapore's most iconic attraction - the Flower Dome (world's largest glass greenhouse) and Cloud Forest (35m indoor waterfall with misty mountain walkways). The Supertree Grove is free to walk around, but the paid conservatories are worth every cent. The OCBC Skyway between the Supertrees is a separate ticket and best visited at sunset. Klook combo tickets save 10-15% versus buying at the door.

3Singapore Night Safari
⭐ 4.5 (15,000+ reviews) ⏱ 3-4 hours 🌙 World's first nocturnal zoo

The world's first nocturnal wildlife park, home to over 900 animals across 100+ species - all viewed in a naturalistic night setting. Take the guided tram ride through different habitat zones, then walk the trails to see fishing cats, Malayan tigers, and giant flying squirrels up close. The Creatures of the Night show is a highlight. Arrive early (7 PM opening) to avoid the longest tram queue.

4S.E.A. Aquarium (Singapore Oceanarium)
⭐ 4.6 (18,000+ reviews) ⏱ 2-3 hours 🐠 Sentosa Island

One of the world's largest aquariums with over 100,000 marine animals across 50 habitats. The centrepiece is the massive Open Ocean viewing panel - 36 metres wide, with manta rays and sharks gliding past. Great for families and rainy days. The aquarium connects directly to Universal Studios, so you can do both in one Sentosa day trip. Klook entry is cheaper and includes a mobile pass - no printing needed.

5Sentosa Luge & Skyride
⭐ 4.6 (12,000+ reviews) ⏱ 1-2 hours 🛷 Family fun

Ride a gravity-powered luge kart down winding tracks on Sentosa, then take the Skyride chairlift back to the top with panoramic views of the harbour and city skyline. Four distinct tracks with different difficulty levels - the Dragon and Expedition trails are the most thrilling. Buy the 4-ride ticket on Klook for the best value. A favourite for both kids and adults, and one of Sentosa's most-repeated activities.

6Singapore Zoo
⭐ 4.6 (10,000+ reviews) ⏱ 4-5 hours 🐘 Open-concept zoo

One of Asia's best zoos, famous for its open-concept enclosures where animals roam in naturalistic habitats with minimal visible barriers. Don't miss the orangutan feeding session, the white tiger exhibit, and the Splash Safari sea lion show. The tram service covers the entire park - useful for families with young children. Best visited at sunset for golden hour views transitioning into city lights. Note: the SkyPark observation deck is separate from the famous infinity pool (hotel guests only). 10Bird Paradise (Mandai Wildlife Reserve)
⭐ 4.5 (4,000+ reviews) ⏱ 3-4 hours 🦜 Asia's largest bird park

The replacement for the beloved Jurong Bird Park, now relocated to the Mandai precinct next to Singapore Zoo. Walk through eight massive aviaries housing 3,500 birds from 400 species - including the stunning Crimson Wetlands, Heart of Africa, and the penguin exhibit. The feeding sessions and bird shows are excellent for families. Combine with Singapore Zoo (next door) for a Mandai double-header.

Getting to Singapore

Singapore Changi Airport (SIN) is one of the world's best-connected airports. From Kuala Lumpur, it's just 1 hour by flight or 4-5 hours by bus. From Hong Kong, about 4.75 hours. Frequently Asked Questions

Is Klook cheaper than buying tickets at the attraction?
For Singapore attractions - almost always yes. Universal Studios, Gardens by the Bay, Night Safari, and most Sentosa attractions are SGD 5-15 cheaper on Klook versus walk-in counter prices. The Klook Attractions Saver Pass bundles 3-5 attractions for an additional 20-40% off. There's no reason to buy at the door anymore.
What are the must-do activities for first-time visitors?
For first-timers, the Big 5 Singapore experiences are: Gardens by the Bay (iconic), Universal Studios (fun), Night Safari (unique), Marina Bay Sands SkyPark (views), and a hawker centre meal (Chinatown Complex or Maxwell Food Centre - free and unforgettable). These cover Singapore's best mix of nature, thrills, wildlife, views, and food.
How much does a Singapore trip cost from Malaysia?
A weekend trip from KL is very affordable. Flights start from MYR 80-150 return (or take a bus for MYR 30-50). Budget hotels from SGD 80/night. Activities on Klook total SGD 100-200 for the main attractions. A 2-3 night trip for two people costs roughly MYR 1,500-3,000 total. Using Klook promo codes and booking bundled tickets saves significantly.
What's the best Sentosa itinerary?
Morning: Universal Studios (arrive at 10 AM opening for shortest queues). Afternoon: S.E.A. Aquarium (air-conditioned break). Late afternoon: Sentosa Luge + Skyride (3-4 rides). Evening: Beach dinner at Coastes or Tanjong Beach Club. Transport: Cable car in, Sentosa Express out. Book all tickets on Klook the night before for the best prices.
Is Singapore good for families with kids?
Singapore is one of Asia's best family destinations. Universal Studios, S.E.A. Aquarium, Singapore Zoo, Bird Paradise, Sentosa Luge, and KidZania are all world-class and designed for families. The city is clean, safe, English-speaking, and easy to navigate by MRT. Most attractions are indoors or covered - important given Singapore's tropical heat and afternoon rain.
