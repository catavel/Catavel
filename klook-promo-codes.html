About Klook

Klook is Asia's leading travel experiences platform, offering 500,000+ activities, tours, and attractions in 2,700+ destinations worldwide. Founded in 2014 and headquartered in Hong Kong, Klook is particularly popular among Singapore and Malaysia travelers for its competitive prices on tours, theme parks, and airport transfers across Asia.
