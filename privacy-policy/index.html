Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data Catavel collects, how we use it, and your rights. We keep things simple because we collect very little.

The short version: Catavel does not require sign-ups, does not store personal data, and does not sell information to third parties. We use Google Analytics to understand how our site is used, and affiliate cookies are set by our travel partners when you click a booking link.

1. Who We Are

Catavel is a travel deal discovery platform operated from Southeast Asia. Our website is catavel.com. For privacy-related questions, you can contact us at hello@catavel.com.

2. What Data We Collect

Catavel collects minimal data. We do not require user accounts, logins, or registrations. Here is exactly what we collect:

Data TypeWhatWhyStored By
Analytics Page views, device type, country, referral source Understand which content is useful Google Analytics (GA4)
Affiliate cookies Tracking ID linking your booking to Catavel Attribute bookings so we earn commission Partner sites (Klook, Agoda, etc.)
Email address Only if you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter Send weekly travel deal digest Mailchimp

We do not collect: names, phone numbers, payment information, passport details, IP addresses (GA4 does not log IP by default), or any other personally identifiable information.

3. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use Catavel. GA4 collects anonymised usage data such as pages visited, time on site, device type, and approximate geographic region. GA4 does not collect IP addresses by default and does not identify individual users.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, or by using a browser with built-in tracking protection.

4. Cookies

Catavel itself does not set cookies. However, the following third-party services may set cookies when you interact with our site:

ServiceCookie PurposeDuration
Google Analytics Distinguish unique visitors, session tracking Up to 2 years
Klook Affiliate tracking (set when you click a Klook link) 30 days
Trip.com Affiliate tracking (set when you click a Trip.com link) 30 days
Agoda Affiliate tracking (set when you click an Agoda link) 30-90 days
Travelpayouts Affiliate tracking for partner OTAs 30 days
Mailchimp Newsletter signup form functionality Session

You can manage or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Blocking affiliate cookies will not affect your ability to browse Catavel, but it will mean we do not earn a commission if you make a booking.

5. Newsletter

If you subscribe to our newsletter, your email address is stored by Mailchimp (operated by Intuit Inc.). We use it solely to send you a weekly travel deal digest. We do not share your email with any other party. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email, or by contacting us directly.

6. Third-Party Links

Catavel contains links to third-party websites (booking platforms, airlines, travel service providers). Once you leave Catavel by clicking an external link, your activity is governed by that website's privacy policy — not ours. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any site where you provide personal or payment information.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties. The only data processing occurs through:

  • Google Analytics: Anonymised usage statistics
  • Mailchimp: Newsletter subscriber emails (only if you opt in)
  • Affiliate partners: Booking attribution via cookies (no personal data is shared by us)

8. Data Retention

Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted. Newsletter subscriber data is retained until you unsubscribe. Catavel does not maintain any other user database.

9. Your Rights

Regardless of where you are located, you have the right to:

  • Access: Ask what data we hold about you (the answer is almost certainly: none)
  • Deletion: Request removal of your newsletter subscription and any associated data
  • Opt out: Disable cookies, opt out of Google Analytics, or unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time
  • Complain: Contact the relevant data protection authority in your jurisdiction if you believe your rights have been violated

For users in Singapore, this policy complies with the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA). For users in the EU, we process minimal data and do not track individual users, which aligns with GDPR principles of data minimisation.

10. Children's Privacy

Catavel is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information (e.g., via the newsletter form), please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We will not reduce your rights under this policy without your explicit consent.
